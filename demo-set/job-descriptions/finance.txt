Position Overview:

We are seeking a skilled and detail-oriented Certified Accountant to join our finance team. As a Certified Accountant, you will play a pivotal role in maintaining accurate financial records, ensuring compliance with regulations, and providing insightful financial analysis. Your expertise in accounting principles and your meticulous attention to financial details will contribute to the financial health and success of our organization.

Responsibilities:

Manage and oversee the organization's financial transactions, including accounts payable, accounts receivable, and general ledger entries.
Prepare accurate and timely financial statements, including balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements.
Perform reconciliations of financial accounts and ensure accuracy and completeness of financial data.
Assist in budgeting and forecasting processes to provide valuable insights for decision-making.
Ensure compliance with relevant accounting standards, regulations, and tax laws.
Collaborate with internal and external auditors to facilitate audits and maintain proper documentation.
Analyze financial data to identify trends, patterns, and opportunities for improvement.
Provide financial reports and analysis to management for informed strategic planning.
Stay updated with changes in accounting standards, regulations, and industry best practices.

Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field; CPA or equivalent certification is required.
Proven experience as an Accountant or in a similar financial role.
Strong understanding of accounting principles, financial regulations, and tax laws.
Proficiency in accounting software and Microsoft Office Suite, especially Excel.
Detail-oriented with exceptional analytical and problem-solving skills.
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to collaborate with cross-functional teams.
Strong organizational skills to manage financial data, records, and reports.
Ability to work independently, meet deadlines, and handle multiple tasks.

Skills Needed:

Expertise in accounting principles and financial reporting standards.
Advanced proficiency in using accounting software and financial tools.
Analytical skills to interpret financial data, identify trends, and provide insights.
Strong attention to detail to ensure accuracy in financial records and reports.
Time management skills to meet deadlines and manage multiple priorities.
Communication skills to convey complex financial information to non-financial stakeholders.
Ethical conduct and integrity in handling financial data and confidential information.
Problem-solving abilities to address financial discrepancies and resolve issues.
Adaptability to evolving accounting standards and changes in regulations.
