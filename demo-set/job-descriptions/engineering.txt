Position Overview:

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Electrical Engineer to join our dynamic engineering team. As an Electrical Engineer, you will play a pivotal role in the design, development, and maintenance of electrical systems and components. Your expertise in electrical engineering, attention to detail, and commitment to excellence will be instrumental in delivering high-quality solutions to our projects.

Responsibilities:

Identify and select appropriate electrical components, such as transformers, circuit breakers, and cables, while considering project specifications and budget constraints.
Create and interpret detailed schematic diagrams, wiring diagrams, and engineering drawings.
Conduct thorough testing and troubleshooting of electrical systems and equipment to identify and resolve issues promptly.
Manage the entire lifecycle of electrical engineering projects, including budgeting, scheduling, and resource allocation.
Collaborate closely with multidisciplinary teams, including mechanical engineers, software engineers, and project managers, to ensure seamless integration of electrical systems.
Prepare and maintain meticulous documentation, including technical reports, project plans, and engineering specifications.
Ensure that all electrical designs and installations meet or exceed relevant safety and regulatory standards.
Stay abreast of emerging technologies and industry trends, and propose innovative solutions and improvements.

Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering or a related field.
Professional Engineer (PE) license is advantageous.
Years of experience in electrical engineering, with a proven track record of successful project execution.
Proficiency in electrical design software (e.g., AutoCAD, EAGLE) and simulation tools.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
Excellent communication and collaboration abilities.
Thorough knowledge of industry standards and regulations.
Keen attention to detail and dedication to delivering high-quality work.

Skills Needed:

Proficiency in utilizing electrical design software for detailed engineering designs.
Strong analytical skills to evaluate and enhance electrical systems.
Creativity to develop innovative solutions and optimize designs.
Meticulous attention to detail to ensure precise and error-free electrical diagrams.
Effective problem-solving abilities to tackle electrical challenges and complexities.
Exceptional time management skills to meet project deadlines and manage tasks efficiently.
Collaborative mindset to work effectively within cross-functional teams.
Adaptability to evolving technologies and engineering advancements.
Outstanding written and verbal communication skills to convey technical information effectively.
