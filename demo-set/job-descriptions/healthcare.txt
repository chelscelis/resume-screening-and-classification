Position Overview:

We are seeking a compassionate and skilled Registered Nurse to join our healthcare team. As a Registered Nurse, you will play a vital role in providing high-quality patient care, assessing health needs, and implementing medical treatments. Your expertise, clinical judgment, and dedication to patient well-being will contribute to the overall success of our healthcare facility.

Responsibilities:

Provide direct patient care, including administering medications, treatments, and procedures.
Perform thorough patient assessments, including physical examinations and medical history reviews.
Collaborate with healthcare team members to create individualized care plans and coordinate patient care.
Monitor patients' conditions, track vital signs, and report any changes to the medical team.
Educate patients and their families about medical conditions, treatment plans, and post-discharge care.
Administer and manage intravenous medications and therapies as prescribed.
Document patient information accurately and maintain detailed medical records.
Respond to medical emergencies, provide first aid, and initiate appropriate interventions.
Adhere to infection control protocols, safety guidelines, and ethical standards.

Qualifications:

Graduation from an accredited nursing program and current RN licensure.
Proven experience as a Registered Nurse, preferably in a healthcare or clinical setting.
Strong knowledge of nursing principles, medical procedures, and patient care standards.
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to interact with patients, families, and medical teams.
Ability to remain calm and make critical decisions during stressful situations.
Knowledge of medical equipment and technology relevant to nursing care.
Basic computer skills for electronic health record (EHR) documentation.

Skills Needed:

Clinical expertise to provide safe and effective patient care.
Strong assessment skills to accurately identify patient needs and changes in conditions.
Compassion and empathy to deliver patient-centered care and support.
Critical thinking and problem-solving abilities to make informed clinical judgments.
Adaptability to handle a variety of patient cases and medical situations.
Strong organizational skills to manage patient records and treatment plans.
Communication skills to educate patients, families, and healthcare teams effectively.
Team collaboration to work cohesively with interdisciplinary healthcare providers.
Commitment to maintaining patient privacy, confidentiality, and ethical standards.
